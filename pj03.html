TEANY JAM

: Homemade Jam Made Of Tea


PROLOGUE

Teany jam is a homemade jam made of black tea and green tea. The word ‘teany’ comes from ‘tea’. The pronunciation is also similar to ‘tiny,’ which means that it is a tiny jam.


Teany jam is for single-person households that had a hard time eating bulk jam. To break out of conventional fruit jams, we are researching and developing a new flavor of jams using tea.


Another feature of teany jam is that it has two different tones. The jam is made of two layers: milk jam as the base added with the main jam.
