COLOR OF SKY

Record The Colors Of The Sky


PROLOGUE

When we were young, we used to look up at the sky. We were amused at the colors of the sky and the shapes of the clouds. But at some point, we lost that brief moment to look up and take in the view. All of a sudden, I thought of recording the colors of the sky every day like a diary. How about taking the time to look up at the sky through the window while walking along the street once in a while?


COS(Color Of Sky) is a brochure that recorded the changes of the color of the sky every day and every time, consisting of 12 books each representing every month of the year.


COS(Color Of Sky) is a series of cards that recorded the changes of the color of the sky every day and every time, consisting of 12 each representing every month of the year. You can use COS(Color Of Sky) cards as postcards and for decorating your interior. August consists of 40 cards in total: 31 COS(Color Of Sky) cards and 9 photo cards.
